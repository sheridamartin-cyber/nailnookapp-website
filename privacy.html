Privacy Policy

Nail Nook — Your Collection, Elevated.

📅 Effective Date: July 1, 2026  ·  Last Updated: July 2026

Welcome to Nail Nook. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how Nail Nook ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and safeguards your information when you use our mobile application (the "App"). By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1 Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

Account Information

  • Email address and password (when you register with email)
  • Name and profile information you choose to provide
  • Google account information if you sign in with Google (name, email, profile photo)

Polish Collection Data

  • Polish names, brands, colors, finishes, and other attributes you enter
  • Photos of your nail polish that you upload
  • Collection status (owned, wishlist, destash) and personal notes
  • Ratings and tags you assign to polishes

Usage Information

  • App usage data and feature interactions
  • Device type and operating system (collected automatically)
  • Crash reports and performance data

2 How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Create and manage your Nail Nook account
  • Store and display your nail polish collection
  • Enable app features including catalog browsing, favorites, and destash functionality
  • Improve the App's performance, features, and user experience
  • Respond to support requests and communicate with you about the App
  • Ensure the security and integrity of the App

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We do not use your data for advertising purposes.

3 Shared and Community Data

Nail Nook includes a shared master polish catalog. When you add a polish to the catalog, that polish's information (name, brand, color, finish, and photo) becomes part of the shared database available to all Nail Nook users. Your personal collection data (status, notes, ratings, personal photos) remains private to your account.

  • Polish data you contribute to the master catalog is visible to all users
  • Your username may be associated with polish entries you contribute
  • Your personal collection details, notes, and ratings are private

4 Photos and User Content

When you upload photos of your nail polish:

  • Photos are stored securely using Google Firebase Storage
  • Photos you associate with your personal collection are private to your account
  • Photos you contribute to shared catalog entries may be visible to other users

Community Photo Submissions

Nail Nook allows users to submit photos to a shared community catalog ("Community Photos"). By submitting a photo, you grant Nail Nook a non-exclusive, royalty-free, worldwide license to display that photo within the app to other users.

All submitted Community Photos are reviewed before being made publicly visible. Nail Nook uses Google Cloud Vision, an automated content moderation service, to screen submissions for inappropriate content. Photos that pass automated screening are then reviewed by a Nail Nook moderator before being approved for display. Photos may be rejected at our discretion without notice.

You retain ownership of any photos you submit. You may not submit photos that you do not have the right to share, or that contain nudity, violence, or other inappropriate content.

Please do not upload photos containing personally identifiable information, faces, or content unrelated to nail polish.

5 Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored using Google Firebase (Firestore and Firebase Storage), which provides industry-standard security protections including encryption in transit and at rest.

  • All data transmitted between the App and our servers is encrypted using TLS
  • Access to your account is protected by your password and/or Google authentication
  • We implement access controls to limit who can access your data

While we take reasonable measures to protect your information, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. We encourage you to use a strong, unique password for your account.

6 Third-Party Services

Nail Nook uses the following third-party services, each with their own privacy practices:

  • Google Firebase — Authentication, database, and file storage (Firebase Privacy Policy)
  • Google Sign-In — Optional authentication method (Google Privacy Policy)
  • Google Cloud Vision API — Automated photo content moderation

7 Children's Privacy

Nail Nook is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at support@nailnookapp.com and we will delete that information.

8 Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access — You can view all data associated with your account within the App
  • Correction — You can edit your profile and collection data at any time
  • Deletion — You can delete individual polishes from your collection at any time. To delete your account and all associated data, contact us at support@nailnookapp.com
  • Export — To request a copy of your data, contact us at support@nailnookapp.com

Note: Polishes you have contributed to the shared master catalog may remain in the catalog after account deletion, as they are part of the community database.

9 Data Retention

We retain your account and collection data for as long as your account is active. If you request account deletion, we will delete your personal data within 30 days, except where we are required to retain it for legal purposes.

10 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make significant changes, we will notify you through the App or by email. Your continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

Questions or Concerns?

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your data, we'd love to hear from you.
