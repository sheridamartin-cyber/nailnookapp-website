Features

Everything your collection deserves

Nail Nook is built for collectors who take their polish seriously.
The elegance and detail you expect is right at your fingertips.

📦
Complete Collection Management
Add, organize, and browse your entire polish collection with detailed info: color family, finish, brand, collection, season, and more.
🔍
Never Buy a Duplicate
Browse our catalog of thousands of polishes before you buy. Know instantly if you already own it. Or something close.
📸
Photo Documentation
Add up to 5 photos per polish. Capture swatches, nail art, and bottle shots so your collection looks as good as it is.
❤️
Favorites & Wishlist
Heart your most loved polishes and build a wishlist for bottles you're hunting. Everything in one organized place.
🏷️
Destash Made Easy
Mark polishes for destash and manage your collection intentionally. Know what you love, let go of what you don't.
✨
Shared Master Catalog
Built on a growing community catalog spanning thousands of shades across the brands you love, with new polishes added all the time.
